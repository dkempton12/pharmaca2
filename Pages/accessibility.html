Our commitment to accessibility

To date, there is no specific language in the Americans with Disabilities Act regarding websites, but at Pharmaca we’re committed to accessibility, diversity and inclusion. It is our goal that all of our guests should be able to easily shop online at pharmaca.com.

That's why we are in the process of making pharmaca.com accessible to those using assistive technologies like a screen reader, a magnifier or voice recognition software. Our end goal is to make Pharmaca everyone's preferred shopping destination for wellness and natural beauty products.

While we continue making accessibility improvements, feel free to reach out to us if you need help or want to provide feedback. You may either:

  • Contact us via email at customercare@pharmaca.com
  • Call Pharmaca Customer Service at 877.328.2680 (Monday-Friday, 9am-4pm MST)
    Voice messages left after hours will be returned as soon as possible during the next business day.
