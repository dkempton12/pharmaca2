About Wishgarden Herbs

Joy is contagious. And what better way to maintain good health? This has been the philosophy at WishGarden Herbal Remedies since master herbalist Catherine Hunziker purchased the company in 1987.

Today, she runs the business with her son Sam and daughter Anna. And their focus hasn’t wavered.

WishGarden’s spirit is seen in every product they make, from their signature and powerful Kick-Ass line of tinctures, to their soothing salves, oils and teas. WishGarden Herbs taps into the incredible healing power of nature, sourcing its herbs from the best organic farms, forests and gardens in America.

This back-to-basics approach allows the company to create unique and authentic formulations that work. WishGarden Herbs also offers products designed specifically for pregnancy, women, and children.
