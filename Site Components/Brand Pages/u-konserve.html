About U•Konserve

U•Konserve was started in 2008 by two moms who became increasingly concerned about the environmental impact of the lunchtime trash at their children’s schools. They set out to create sustainably designed food-storage products to reduce the disposable food packaging responsible for so much of the landfill trash.

The company now offers a complete line of reusable food-storage solutions for lunch, work, travel, picnics, takeout, family leftovers and more. Products include leak-proof stainless steel containers, glass containers and water bottles with protective silicone sleeves, insulated food jars, reusable sandwich wraps and snack bags, bamboo utensils, recycled lunch totes and stainless steel straws.

U•Konserve products empower people to make a difference every day. One reusable solution can save thousands of single-use plastic bags and containers from landfills. Replacing disposables with long-lasting alternatives is a commitment to foster this important change. In much the same way that a small personal choice can have a big impact, U•Konserve also believes that the impact of a small company can have a powerful and lasting effect on the health of the environment.

About U•Konserve Products

Reusable and Waste-free

  • Designed to reduce the staggering amount of trash that ends up in our landfills and oceans every year because of single-use, disposable food packing.

Made from High-Quality, Durable, Safe Materials

  • Stainless steel is 304-grade (18/8) and is unlined, non-toxic, rust and stain resistant, and recyclable
  • Glass resists stains, scratches and odors, and is recyclable and sustainable
  • Plastics are non-toxic PET #1, LDPE #4 and PP #5
  • Silicone is food-grade and non-toxic
  • PET fabrics are made from 100% recycled plastic bottles
  • All products meet CPSIA standards and are free of BPA, phthalates and lead
  • Long-lasting products are sustainably designed to minimize waste throughout each product’s life cycle

Functional and Practical for the Whole Family

  • Products for kids and adults, ideal for school lunches, work, travel, picnics, takeout, salad bars, and family leftovers
  • Easy to clean, easy to pack and easy to store
  • Dishwasher safe

Responsibility

U•Konserve is dedicated to sustainable and ethical business practices, and promotes environmental and personal health in a variety of ways.

  • U•Konserve is a certified B Corporation, and has met rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, accountability and transparency. This third-party certification demonstrates U•Konserve’s long-standing pledge to support the environment.
  • U•Konserve is a member of 1% for the Planet, donating 1 percent of their total sales to environmental organizations that share their mission.
  • U•Konserve offers non-toxic, safe products that are tested by CPSIA-accredited independent labs to ensure that they are free of BPA, phthalates and lead.
  • U•Konserve offers high-quality, long-lasting products that are sustainably designed to minimize waste throughout each product's life cycle.
  • U•Konserve offers products that are responsibly made in China, India and Taiwan in facilities that are working toward green practices, and supportive of their mission to reduce waste.
  • U•Konserve actively educates customers about sustainability and shares resources about personal and environmental health.
  • U•Konserve’s team culture is in line with their mission to reduce waste—allowing them to advocate for sustainability and educate customers based on their own experiences and knowledge.
  • U•Konserve offers discounts and advice for schools, and donates 20% of sales back to schools that help promote waste-free lunch packaging by offering our products.
  • U•Konserve received a grant from Eileen Fisher for Women Entrepreneurs that helped grow their business. They pay it forward by giving back to organizations that support their mission, including Healthy Child, Healthy World, Cool the Earth and Grades of Green.
