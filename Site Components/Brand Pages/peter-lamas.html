About Peter Lamas

Beauty without compromise

What’s natural to you? At Peter Lamas, there are strong feelings about what we apply to our skin and how we take care of our hair.

Peter Lamas believes in the purity of natural extracts, the benefit of exotic oils, the wide-ranging properties of botanicals, and the potency of active ingredients, vitamins and herbs to enhance your skin and hair. When combined with science and technology, their products deliver beautiful results.

Peter Lamas products are 100% vegan and incorporate certified organic botanicals, without parabens, sulfates or harmful chemicals. Peter Lamas does not use any animal byproducts and never tests on animals. They believe in delivering stunning results with healthy, good-for-you ingredients. Peter Lamas believes in excellence. That’s what’s natural to them.

What makes Peter Lamas products unique?

Peter Lamas strives to combine the best of nature with healthy science in their product formulations. Each of their products is created using a balance of active natural ingredients and advanced skin and hair care science. Peter Lamas formulates their products using potent levels of healthy ingredients like peptides, apple stem cells, retinol, resveratrol and hyaluronic acid to give you visible and lasting results. They offer products that sit at the crossroads of natural bio-nutrients that deliver clinical-strength results for skin and hair.

The man behind the brand

Peter Lamas is the founder and chief product formulator of Peter Lamas Beauty. More than 30 years ago, Cuban born Peter Lamas began his career at Vidal Sassoon in New York City. Peter trained directly under Sassoon, and quickly became a go-to stylist for classic beauties such as Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis, Grace Kelly, Audrey Hepburn and Diana Ross. Upon moving to the Henri Bendel Salon with friend, Paul Mitchell, Lamas was given the opportunity to work closely with the greatest fashion photographers of our time, such as Richard Avedon, Irving Penn and Francesco Scavullo for editorial photo shoots at Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, Glamour and Mademoiselle, as well as major fashion runway shows and national print advertising.

Peter Lamas Beauty was founded with the overriding mission to stay ahead of the curve, using the most innovative technologies the industry has to offer to formulate premium hair care, skin care, body care and anti-aging products. Through in-depth research, experience and a passion to create amazing beauty products that make a positive difference for people, Peter Lamas has become a leading international beauty expert, regarded by consumers and experts alike as an innovative leader within the beauty industry.
