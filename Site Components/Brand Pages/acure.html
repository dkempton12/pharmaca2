CHLORELLA GROWTH FACTOR (CGF)

Chlorella is a single-cell green algae, and is rich in RNA and DNA, the nucleic acids that create life. Our bodies’ creation of RNA and DNA significantly declines in our early twenties, which leads to aging. Healthy cells count on the perfect replication of our RNA and DNA, and CGF helps boost this process and protect our DNA and RNA during the replication process. Acure’s CGF contains 10,000 mg per 100 g of RNA, the highest source available for your skin.

CGF is also clinically proven to protect collagen and elastin fibers from the enzymes that destroy them. In fact, clinical studies have shown CGF to increase collagen expression by 333% when used at 1% concentration. Acure’s anti-aging products include CGF at an amazing 5% concentration.

CUROXIDANT BLEND

The curoxidant blend is a combination of five certified organic superfruits and herbs that are freeze-dried and carefully blended to ensure maximum potency. Acure’s Curoxidant Blend effectively neutralizes free radicals and helps reverse the signs of aging. You can find Curoxidant in all of Acure’s products.

Certified Organic Acai
Widely used by people in Central and South America for traditional medicinal purposes due to its ability to fight infection. Acai is now recognized worldwide for its potent antioxidant properties. It’s also high in omega fatty acids and anthocyanins.

Certified Organic Pomegranate
Popular for its high antioxidant content, pomegranate is now being noticed for its strong anti-inflammatory properties. A University of Michigan Medical School study, published in the February 2006 Journal of Ethnopharmacology, showed that pomegranate seed oil stimulates keratinocyte proliferation, promoting regeneration and strengthening of the epidermis. Pomegranate is also one of the only plant sources of Conjugated Linoleic Acid or CLA.

Certified Organic Blackberry
This fantastic antioxidant berry is rich in tannins, gallic acid and high in vitamin C.

Certified Organic Rosehips
The fruit of the rose bush, rosehips is one of the most powerful sources of natural vitamin C. Also high in vitamins A, D, E, flavonoids and lycopene.

Certified Organic Rooibos
This red tea is known for high antioxidant content. Rooibos is also an effective skin care ingredient due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory abilities.
