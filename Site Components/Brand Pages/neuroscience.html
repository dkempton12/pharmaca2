About Neuroscience Products

Kavinace

Contains a blend of ingredients involved in the synthesis and activity of GABA, a neurotransmitter important for sleep*

GABA is the primary inhibitory neurotransmitter in the brain, meaning its central role is to balance excitability of neurons. Because of its function, GABA is important for promoting sleep, and calm and managing stress. Stress is thought to alter GABA synthesis and activity by affecting the expression of GABA-synthesizing enzymes and receptors.

The addition of ingredients such as taurine and vitamin B6 are important for glutamate-GABA balance.* High glutamate can damage neurons, alter cellular homeostasis and cause mitochondrial dysfunction, which can ultimately lead to cell death. Taurine demonstrates GABA-A agonist activity and protects neurons and mitochondria against glutamate-induced damage.* The active form of vitamin B6 is important for the synthesis of GABA as it is a required co-factor for glutamate decarboxylase, the enzyme responsible for converting glutamate to GABA.*
