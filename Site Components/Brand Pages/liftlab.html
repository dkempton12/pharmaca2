About LIFTLAB

One of the most innovative brands on the skin care market, LIFTLAB merges natural ingredients with cutting-edge technology. Twenty-five years ago LIFTLAB’s founding scientists discovered how plants and animals survive harsh Arctic and desert conditions: They produce a unique family of “Cell Protection Proteins” (CPP) that protect them from the damaging conditions of cold, extreme dryness, heat and the UV rays of the sun.

Using medical research, the company explored how CPP can be used to improve skin care products and found that CPP boosts skin’s natural production of 41 essential proteins that protect and rejuvenate, including antioxidants, collagen, elastin and the proteins that help skin to heal.

Now LIFTLAB is the go-to brand for battling aging and creating youthful-looking skin. They’ve received many an editorial accolade from the likes of Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, New Beauty, as well as from journals for dermatologists and plastic surgeons.

Users will experience an immediate difference, but maximum results come in 30–60 days; that’s because LIFTLAB products don’t just cover your skin, they work with your skin as new cells form.

All LIFTLAB products are free from artificial fragrance and colors, sulfates and parabens.

The science behind LIFTLAB

Anti-aging skin care powered by nature, delivered by science.

Naturally found in plants and animals who live in harsh climates, LIFTLAB’s Cell Protection Protein helps to protect and to promote skin's natural ability for self-repair and regeneration.

Because visible signs of aging are the result of cellular breakdown from age and the environment, using CPP on the skin counteracts these effects, essentially "undoing" the aging process.

Backed by 25 years of medical research, LIFTLAB products increase skin firmness, elasticity and moisture, resulting in younger and healthier looking skin.

What is Cell Protection Protein

Twenty-five years ago the founding scientists of LIFTLAB Skincare discovered how plants and animals survive the harsh cold of the Arctic and extreme heat of the desert: they produce a unique protein that protects them from environmental dangers and helps them heal more quickly than their counterparts elsewhere in the world—an antifreeze protein called Cell Protection Protein, or CPP for short.
