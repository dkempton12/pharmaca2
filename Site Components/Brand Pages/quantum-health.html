About Quantum Health

For more than 35 years Quantum Health has led the natural products industry in developing supplements and dermal care products that truly work. From the start, the focus was to source only the highest quality, research-supported ingredients to ensure product effectiveness. Their willingness to invest time and care at the outset has translated into one loyal customer base.

Rooted in the Natural World

While their products are distributed all over the US and Canada, Quantum Health is still a family-owned company with deep roots in Eugene, Ore. The company’s headquarters is less than an hour’s drive to the mountains to the east or the Pacific coast to the west, and the team—from the CEO and product developers to our sales team and customer support staff—is inspired by the power of nature to keep the body in balance. At the end of any workweek, that inspiration is waiting, from the nature preserves within city limits to the national forests just outside of town.
