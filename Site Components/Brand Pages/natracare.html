About Natracare

Natracare is a specialist and pioneer in organic and natural personal care solutions. Established in 1989, Natracare was created as a direct response to concerns about the serious environmental and health issues surrounding conventional feminine hygiene manufacturing.

Natracare is the world’s leading manufacturer of organic and natural feminine hygiene products including tampons, pads, panty liners and wipes as well as care for new mothers. The range offers women reliable and comfortable monthly protection, free from plastics, dioxins, perfumes and pesticides, and works just like conventional products.

In addition, all of Natracare’s feminine hygiene and baby care products are made with only organic and natural materials that meet the highest organic, environmental and biodegradable standards, sourced from raw material producers that manage and monitor resources respectfully and share their principles.

The Natracare brand philosophy has always been to provide products that never compromise health or the environment. Many brands of sanitary protection contain crude oil plastics, synthetics, harsh chemicals and even dioxin residues, which can cause intimate irritation for women, and 1000s of years of pollution for our environment. All Natracare tampons, pads, liners and wipes are Totally Chlorine Free (TCF) and free of plastic and crude oil derivatives.

Natracare holds a Green accreditation officiated by the Nordic Ecolabel organization, and is the first feminine hygiene brand to have obtained an International Environmental Product Declaration (a scientifically validated, independently assessed life-cycle analysis that determines the carbon footprint of the products).

Susie Hewson, founder and developer of Natracare, was awarded the 2008 Women in Ethical Business award. The entire Natracare line has been awarded the LOHAS award for its environmental and sustainable criteria and has the highest ethical Accreditation Index Score, a fully independent endorsement that outlines the ‘premier league’ of genuine corporate social responsibility and ethical companies.

Natracare Product Info

Natracare Tampons

Natracare tampons were the world’s first fully certified organic cotton tampons. Made using only certified organic 100% cotton and nothing else, they are Totally Chlorine Free (TCF), contain no rayon, plastics, perfumes or dyes and are GMO free.

Certified organic to the Global Organic Textiles Standard by The Soil Association, an independent U.K certification body which is also USDA NOP accredited. Organic certification of Natracare products also ensures that they meet the highest and most robust standards possible in animal welfare, environment and wildlife protection.

Packaged in recycled card boxes, Natracare tampons are biodegradable and compostable. Available in regular, super and super plus absorbencies and both applicator and non-applicator styles.

Natracare Maxi Pads

Natracare Maxi Pads are thick, long, cushioned pads for secure comfort, great fit and effective absorption, and the soft 100% organic cotton cover allows the skin to breathe. Available in regular, super and longer nighttime absorbencies.

Natracare Ultra Pads

Natracare Ultra Pads are slim and very discreet with the added security of wings. The absorbent core absorbs the menstrual fluid and locks it away so that the soft organic cotton cover allows your skin to breathe, whilst at the same time keeping you dry. Available in regular, super, super plus and long absorbencies.

Natracare Ultra Extra Pads

Natracare Ultra Extra sanitary pads offer a whole new level of performance and quality. The ultra-thin style has the added security of wings, and double layers of protection for high performance absorption and extra comfort.

Hygienically individually wrapped in a TCF paper pouch. Available in normal, super and long absorbencies.

Natracare Panty Liners

You get only soft, certified 100% organic cotton next to your skin when using Natracare panty liners, and no exposure to perfume, plastic or synthetics. Just totally chlorine free, natural plant-based materials that are breathable and soft, to keep you comfortable and dry for everyday protection.

Natracare panty liners are biodegradable and compostable and packaged in recycled card. USDA Biobased certified and Vegetarian Society approved. Available in 6 different styles:

  • Mini: A straight edged liner in mini style that fits discreetly in your underwear. Pack contains 30 liners.
  • Curved: Anatomically contoured for extraordinary comfort. Each pack contains 30 liners.
  • Tanga: Designed for use with string or thong underwear. Each pack contains 30 liners.
  • Ultra Thin: Natracare ultra-thin panty liners are comfortable and discreet. 22 liners per pack.
  • Normal & Long: Anatomically contoured and individually wrapped panty liners in normal and long styles. 18 normal liners and 16 long liners per pack.

Natracare Organic Cotton Makeup Removal Wipes

Natracare’s COSMOS-certified organic makeup removal wipes, the first of their kind, are designed to gently cleanse and moisturize the skin by removing makeup, including waterproof mascara and lipstick. The soft, strong organic cotton cloth is generous in size to properly cleanse the skin, but the natural and organic ingredients leave the skin moisturized and refreshed naturally.

Formulated for sensitive skin, the wipes are dermatologically and ophthalmologically tested. They do not contain any palm oil-derived ingredients, are synthetic free and never tested on animals. They are suitable for vegans and are Vegetarian Society approved. Packaged in a resealable pocket, each pack contains 20 wipes.

Natracare Organic Cotton Intimate Wipes

Soft cleansing organic intimate wipes are ideal for daily intimate hygiene and convenient while travelling. The soft, strong, certified organic 100% cotton wipes are infused with certified organic essential oils of chamomile, calendula and French rose as well as natural plant extracts, which work together to cleanse and refresh, leaving the skin softened.

The gentle formula is free from alcohol, detergents such as sodium lauryl sulfates (SLS and any of its close relatives), parabens, SHG and other types of toxic, formaldehyde-releasing preservatives. Packaged in a resealable pocket, each pack contains 12 wipes.
