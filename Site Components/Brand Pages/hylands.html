About Hyland's

Hyland’s began more than 100 years ago as a small downtown Los Angeles homeopathic health service, and now manufactures products for stores throughout North America. Since the company’s inception, the people of Hyland’s and its parent company, Standard Homeopathic Company, have worked to make safe and natural medicines accessible to everyone.

Standard Homeopathic Company was founded in 1903 by eight Los Angeles doctors, who hired George Hyland as the Company’s first pharmacist. This initiated a tradition of placing health at the forefront of the business, a tradition that still continues. Today, the Company maintains a whole team of pharmacists and health care professionals to help formulate great medicines and meet the needs of consumers.

The Company is proud of its full line of homeopathic solutions for the entire family. Hyland’s unique quick-dissolving tablets were developed in the 1950s by then-owner Cecil Craig for his own daughter, and have become a must-have solution for parents and are a key reason for Hyland’s success in pediatric care.

Hyland’s also boasts adult solutions that include Calms Forté to help with sleep; arnica-based pain relievers for post-workout relief; top-selling Hyland’s Leg Cramps; and Hyland’s DEFEND, a complete line of cold and cough medicines.

Today, Standard is poised to build on its past success with continued sales growth, exciting new product introductions and furthering its generations-long connection with those seeking effective, gentle medicines for their families.
