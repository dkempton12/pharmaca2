LELO Product Info

Production
Each and every item is assembled by hand in LELO's fully owned production facility, ensuring LELO maintains its proud reputation for the highest quality standards. From materials sourcing through to product testing, LELO leaves nothing to chance in its determination to offer the world's best intimate lifestyle products.

Materials
LELO's renowned attention to safety begins with choosing only the best materials and components for every product. From FDA-approved and independently tested silicone to the industry's first-ever Lithium Ion batteries capable of more than 6,000 charges, LELO massagers are built for years of safe, satisfying enjoyment.

Design
LELO designs are inspired in many ways, with the worlds of art, architecture, nature and anatomy informing the look and feel valued by millions around the world. Meanwhile, function follows form to its most intuitive effect, with every consideration taken in crafting thoughtfully designed pleasure products that suit every taste.

Quality assurance/testing
LELO products are subjected to rigorous testing at various stages of manufacturing, ensuring the highest safety and quality standards are met. Items on the production line are selected at random and put through a battery of tests for power capacity, noise levels, waterproofing, drop and trauma testing—because when it comes to quality, LELO leaves nothing to chance.

Eco-friendly Products
LELO prides itself on offering the most eco-friendly designs on the market, made with efficient production processes and internationally recognized environmental protection standards. Every aspect of a LELO product is made with an eye to reducing environmental impact, with an ISO 9001 certified manufacturing process that is WEE-compliant. These guidelines are upheld in every area of operation.

Complete Peace of Mind
A LELO product is destined to take up a very special place in the life of a customer—which is why LELO quality extends far beyond the shop floor. Every item is covered by a full 1-year warranty and a 10-year quality guarantee for pleasure and enjoyment for years to come.
