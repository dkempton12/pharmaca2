About Organic India

At the heart of ORGANIC INDIA is a commitment to being a living embodiment of love and consciousness in action. They work with thousands of small family farmers in India to cultivate tens of thousands of acres of sustainable organic farmland.

ORGANIC INDIA’s Tulsi Teas and herbal supplements are ethically grown and wildcrafted by small family farmers in India using regenerative agriculture practices that go beyond organic standards and restore vibrant health to the land. ORGANIC INDIA empowers farmers through fair wages and education, cultivating holistic practices that promote the health of the planet through soil health and fertility, improved water retention and carbon sequestering.

Their commitment to regenerative agriculture is interwoven with a commitment to revitalizing communities by means of social programming initiatives, access to clean water, health care and job skills training that empower and support some of India’s most vulnerable populations.

Each ORGANIC INDIA product is one link in a chain of connectedness between Mother Nature, farmers and you. By choosing ORGANIC INDIA you are completing this chain, actively participating in their mission to create a healthier environment, providing a life of dignity to our farmers, and bringing health and happiness to you.

About Organic India Products

Tulsi Teas

Revered in India as “The Queen of Herbs,” tulsi, also known as holy basil, is an adaptogenic herb that has been used for more than 5,000 years to support immunity and a healthy stress response, in addition to a variety of other health benefits.* Organic Tulsi Teas contain three sacred varieties of tulsi (rama, vana and krishna), providing a delicious and nourishing herbal infusion.

Supplements

ORGANIC INDIA’s unique, organically grown and ethically produced herbal supplement line is designed to be both gentle and effective for everyone. They make it easy to choose a health-conscious lifestyle with its vegan capsules and smoothie-friendly powders. Elevate your day with the newest line of Lift products in pre-portioned pouches for a convenient on-the-go option.

About Organic India Certifications

B Corp

Certified B Corporations are for-profit companies that have been thoroughly evaluated by B Lab—a third party, non-profit organization—on social and environmental performance, accountability and transparency. Started in 2006, B Corp certifies purpose-driven businesses that create benefit for all stakeholders, not just shareholders.

ORGANIC INDIA is dedicated to a holistic business modality that serves people and the planet. As a business, their goal is to add more value than what they pull out, be it from the soil, farmers or employees.

Fairtrade America

Fairtrade is a global movement for change, working to empower small-scale farmers and workers marginalized by trade. Choosing Fairtrade America products is a simple way to support the people growing the things we love to build a better future for themselves, their families and their communities. ORGANIC INDIA believes in paying a fair wage and providing a life of dignity for our farmers.

Non-GMO Project Verification

The Non-GMO Project is North America’s most trusted third-party verification for non-GMO food and products. The organization dedicates resources to raising awareness and encouraging government to allow consumers to make informed decisions about purchasing GMO products. All of ORGANIC INDIA’s Tulsi teas and herbal supplements have been verified as Non-GMO. This verification authenticates the integrity of ORGANIC INDIA seeds, herbs and products as natural, heritage strains. Businesses must renew their verification every year to ensure compliance with the most recent and relevant certification standards.

USDA Organic Certification

The USDA Organic certification verifies that all United States Department of Agriculture regulations, requirements and specifications are adhered to. It covers a wide range of detailed monitoring and control measures. It is one of the most comprehensive and demanding certification systems to ensure absolute commitment to Organic Certification qualifications as established by the United States Department of Agriculture.

All of ORGANIC INDIA’s Tulsi teas and herbal supplements are USDA Certified Organic. We work with thousands of small family farmers in India to cultivate tens of thousands of acres of sustainable organic farmland, providing resources and education to become certified.

OU Kosher

The OU logo indicates that a product may be consumed by all those who observe kosher dietary laws, as well as by many others who have special dietary requirements. By far the largest of the kosher certification agencies, the Orthodox Union is unmatched for reliability and for meeting the highest kosher standards. When a product is certified Kosher, the consumer can be assured that every ingredient going into that product is identified and sourced with the highest integrity.

Halal Certification

The Halal Committee (JUM) reviews products, ingredients, processing techniques and sanitation procedures to ensure compliance with Halal with specific reference to Islamic dietary laws. JUM enjoys worldwide recognition as the only national body from India authorized to perform Halal Certification of Indian Products.
