About Babo Botanicals

Babo is the name of founder Kate Solomon's son's security bunny, who continues to comfort and soothe him while he sleeps. Kate and her family have really come to depend on Babo! "My mission is to provide the highest quality, purest hair, scalp and skin care products for babies and kids. Both my personal and professional experiences have contributed to my inspiration for the line," says Kate.

Kate draws from 10 years of experience in product development for beauty companies such as L'Oreal, Givenchy and Avon. After the birth of her first child, it seemed to her that most baby "grooming" products were long on chemical names and didn't truly address children's particular hair and skin problems. Kate sought to develop a line of botanical products that offer parents the same level of choice they value in selecting products for themselves.

Fresh solutions based on proven plant science Several years ago Kate started down a path that would ultimately combine her expertise in hair care with her passion for natural ingredients. Kate began working with top botanists to develop a line of products that reflect the attributes she values for her own children — sulfate free and allergy-tested, containing certified organic ingredients and developed using plant-based science. Kate also worked with hair stylists from top Manhattan salons to ensure product performance and professional quality.
