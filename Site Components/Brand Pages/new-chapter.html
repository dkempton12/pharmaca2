About New Chapter

New Chapter was founded in 1982 by Paul and Barbi Schulick. New Chapter first sprang from Paul and Barbi's deep commitment to promote health through innovative formulations made with whole herbs and foods. Mother Nature is a powerful force, and New Chapter's humble mission is to Deliver the Wisdom of Nature to all people interested in natural wellness.

The Whole-Food Difference

New Chapter believes the most appropriate way to deliver nutrients is through the infinite complexity of whole food. To deliver the full profile of nature's bounty to you, they culture vitamins, minerals and herbs in a matrix of organic fruits and vegetables and live probiotics.

The nutrients are transformed into whole-food complexes for your body's greatest benefit. In fact, New Chapter takes a whole-food approach to every formulation, whether it's whole-food complexed multivitamins, whole fish oil, whole-food plant-based calcium or whole herbs and herbal extracts.

Guided by Nature, Validated by Science

While they always look first to nature to guide product formulations, New Chapter relies just as heavily on modern science to direct their selection and use of ingredients. Wide-ranging research in the field helps New Chapter's scientific team seek out even greater discoveries in supporting optimal nourishment and health.

For example, New Chapter's herbals are delivered using innovative supercritical CO2 extraction, a gentle process that helps create a broad-spectrum yet powerfully concentrated herbal extract without the use of high heat.

New Chapter's Commitment to Sustainability

Organic Whenever Possible

New Chapter® has been working toward an organic, sustainable world since their inception in 1982. Their guiding principles include advancing the organic mission and nourishing body and soul with the natural intelligence of pure whole foods. That's why many New Chapter vitamin, mineral, and herbal supplements are made with organic vegetables and herbs. New Chapter believes the natural purity of organic ingredients is better for people and better for the environment.

Proud Non-GMO Advocates

New Chapter has also long been committed to avoiding genetically modified organisms (GMOs) when possible. More than 85 percent of New Chapter products have already been granted verified status by the Non-GMO Project. In fact, New Chapter was the very first vitamin and supplement company to be Non-GMO Project verified.

Philanthropic Efforts

New Chapter is dedicated to several philanthropic efforts and organizations both at home in Vermont and across the globe. For example, New Chapter supplies the Bumi Sehat birthing clinics in Indonesia with Perfect Prenatal multivitamins to fulfill their supplement needs. New Chapter is also a proud partner with the Children's Eternal Rainforest in Costa Rica, helping to raise funds to purchase rainforest land and protect its priceless natural treasures.

Biodynamcic® Partner Farm

New Chapter's partner farm, Finca Luna Nueva, is nestled near the Children's Eternal Rainforest in Costa Rica. Luna Nueva is a Demeter®-certified Biodynamic® farm and is dedicated to organic and sustainable practices that ensure every aspect of farm life thrives in natural harmony. Luna Nueva and its mission have deep meaning for New Chapter and help illustrate and inspire New Chapter's mission of sustainability.
