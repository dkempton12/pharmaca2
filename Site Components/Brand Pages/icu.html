About ICU Eyewear

With each pair of reading glasses sold, ICU Eyewear is making strides toward sustainability in green production and vision health.

Committed to optimal excellence

ICU Eyewear combines uncompromising attention to optical details and premium quality materials and finishes with innovative and artistic design elements.

Their innovative frame styles feature:

  • High-quality frame materials
  • Uncompromising attention to fit, specialty finishes and detailing
  • Quality integrated spring hinges for comfort

Their aspheric lenses are:

  • Made from top-quality materials guaranteed for clarity and magnification accuracy
  • Optimally aligned for ease of use in up-close reading and activities
  • Available in six optically correct strengths: +1.25, +1.50 +1.75, +2.0, +2.50, +3.0

Stylish sustainability

Following a recent corporate evaluation, ICU recognized that they could do more to foster sustainable business practices and reduce their environmental impact—which they did by increasing the amount of eco-friendly production materials used. They also earned CarbonNeutral® certification for both the Eco product line and the company, and switched to 100% recycled packaging printed with soy-based ink.

They continue to push the role of unique materials, such as sustainably harvested bamboo, reclaimed plastics and recycled metals while staying ahead of the fashion curve with styles that showcase the emerging trends from Milan, Paris, New York and Japan. Women can explore new and unconventional frame shapes, matte finishes, bright color blocking, high-contrast black and whites and neutral-shaded patterns. And men will find updated classic frame shapes—featuring mixed materials like metal, bamboo, acetate and plastic—in blues, neutral sandy tones, metallic and tortoises.
