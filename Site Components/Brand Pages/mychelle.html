About MyChelle

The pioneer and authority in advanced, bioactive skin care, MyChelle transformed the natural beauty industry by being the first to use anti-aging peptides, plant stem cells and clinically proven dermatological ingredients.

With natural products now the mainstream expectation, MyChelle’s progressive 360-degree approach to beauty is to provide clean, conscious and comprehensive skin care products that are bioactive, ethically sourced and formulated with high-performance ingredients for superior results and a lifetime of natural, healthy skin.

Founded in 2000 in Colorado, MyChelle’s sustainable manufacturing practices and charitable pursuits support a full line of highly effective targeted treatments including cleansers, moisturizers, serums, nourishing creams and sun protection. All MyChelle products are vegetarian, cruelty-free, and free of phthalates, parabens, sulfates, ureas, artificial fragrances and colors, petroleum, GMOs and gluten.
