About Healthforce Superfoods

HealthForce Superfoods is an independently owned company founded in 1990 by Dr. Jameth Sheridan (D.H.M). HealthForce is committed to providing the best superfood and therapeutic herbal formulas possible. Toward this end, they package their products in unique EcoFresh amber glass bottles. Their amber glass bottles have a metal lid and rubber seal with a quality oxygen absorber in the bottle, which protects superfoods and herbs from oxidation. The amber glass also absorbs damaging wavelengths of light.

HealthForce sets new standards for product purity as well. They use 100 percent whole food ingredients–no fillers, binders, excipients (flow agents), synthetics or isolates. In addition, HealthForce uses organic/wildcrafted materials whenever possible. Many products adhere to the TruGanic standard, which requires actual testing to verify zero trace residues of pesticides, herbicides or GMOs.

Finally, all HealthForce products are 100 percent vegan, and all ingredients in their superfood line are dried at low temperatures to protect the integrity of fragile nutrients. HealthForce is not “just another supplement company.” They truly care about your health and strive to make the best possible products, offering them at the best possible values.
