About Goddess Garden

To soothe her daughter’s sensitive skin, Goddess Garden Founder and CEO Nova Covington began making all her family’s skin care in her kitchen. She used only all-natural, food-grade botanical ingredients that were effective and safe for her daughter. She felt if her family needed clean products, other families did as well. So together with her husband, Paul, Goddess Garden was created.

Because they feel people should spend more time enjoying life and less time reading labels, Nova, Paul and the Goddess Garden team make products that are free from synthetic chemicals that can cause skin reactions, hormone disruption and contribute to the destruction of the coral reefs. Behind every decision they make is a set of guiding core values that ensure their business leaves a minimal footprint and a positive impact.

In addition to being a Certified B Corporation® and a WBENC-certified woman-owned business, Goddess Garden products are certified organic, biodegradable, reef safe, Leaping Bunny-certified cruelty free, vegan, non-nano and non-GMO.

Goddess Garden is most commonly known for its organic, reef-safe, non-nano mineral sunscreen, but they also offer a complete line of certified-organic facial care, essential oils and essential-oil perfumes. Plus, all products feature recyclable packaging!

Goddess Garden Product Information

Goddess Garden Natural Mineral Sunscreens

These certified organic and reef-safe sunscreens use naturally occurring zinc and titanium for long-lasting and powerful sun protection that’s extra gentle on skin. Sunscreens are either unscented or scented with only pure essential oil and they contain no synthetic fragrances or chemicals.

Goddess Garden Essential Oil Perfume

Thousands of mystery chemicals can be hidden under the term “fragrance.” Goddess Garden perfumes are made with pure essential oils, and they list all ingredients so you know exactly what you’re wearing. Now, people with sensitivities to fragrances—or those who want to avoid the mystery—have options!

Goddess Garden Daily Facial Routine

This line offers a plant-powered solution to wrinkles, roughness and dark spots associated with premature aging caused by the sun. By protecting the skin, repairing the damage, and providing the vitamins, nutrients and hydration the sun can deplete, you can rethink retinol—and its harsh side effects—and enjoy younger-looking skin.

Goddess Garden Lip Balm with SPF 30

These lip balms contain a nourishing blend of oils, butters and plant-based waxes to protect and soothe lips, while natural minerals provide powerful SPF 30 sun protection. The certified organic, natural mineral formula is reef-safe, vegan and beeswax-free. Plus, they come in three unique flavors: Lavender Mint, Orange Vanilla and Raspberry.
