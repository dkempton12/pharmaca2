About Herb Pharm

Herb Pharm was established in March of 1979 in a pristine mountain valley in Williams, Ore. From the beginning, their mission has been unwavering:

“We are committed to providing the highest quality herbal products possible, educating people on the safe and effective use of medicinal herbs and inspiring a love for plants and respect for nature.”

Back in the early days, Herb Pharm’s "lab" was the home of Ed Smith and Sara Katz, the company's founders and owners. They wanted to establish themselves as the alternative to the rather poor-quality and limited array of medicinal herb products available. Ed and Sara spoke constantly and emphatically of the importance of using organic, high-quality herbs as opposed to the over-dried plant fiber that the few existing companies were using in their products.

Today at Herb Pharm

Ed and Sara still own and are involved in the daily operations of Herb Pharm, which employs more than 85 people. These operations now include a certified organic herb farm (called the "Pharm Farm").

Herb Pharm’s certified organic farm is only two miles from their modern manufacturing facility, enabling control over every aspect of production, from the soil and seed, to the herb extraction, to the finished product shipped to customers. Herb Pharm hand-harvests herbs at their peak potency, and within minutes delivers them, fresh and succulent, to the manufacturing facility for prompt extraction. When it comes to herbs they can't grow themselves, they purchase directly from other certified organic herb farms whose owners they personally know and trust.

Attention to Detail

Ed and Sara are as picky as ever about the plants they use, as well as their meticulous extraction. Herb Pharm extracts each herb according to its own unique physical and biochemical characteristics at an FDA-inspected, state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. Herb Pharm is continually looking for ways to improve the quality of their herbal medicines.

Herb Pharm’s broad-spectrum extracts seek to mirror the flavor, aroma and natural plant chemistry found in nature. This stands in stark contrast to the ever-increasing trend toward isolating plant constituents, thus modifying their natural array and balance. Herb Pharm is committed to creating herbal extracts that represent the whole plant, and to the fundamental principles of traditional herbal medicine.

Herb Pharm Product Information

Liquid Herbal Extracts

Liquid herbal extracts are easily absorbed and easily dosed, which makes them both popular and highly effective. Simply add drops to water, juice or tea and enjoy.

These liquid herbal extracts are prepared in accordance with official pharmacopeia standards using certified organic alcohol, distilled water and vegetable glycerin. Each herb is extracted to its own unique biochemical profile so that it is rich in the aroma, flavor and bio-activity of the original plant itself.

Herb Pharm’s liquid herbal extracts are available as single herbs, condition-specific multi-herb compounds and as alcohol-free glycerites suitable for children and those with dietary restrictions.

Vegetarian Herbal Capsules

Herb Pharm believes the best herbal ingredients deliver better health results, so our vegetarian capsules and softgels are made from the purest botanicals the world has to offer.

Herb Pharm’s capsules contain concentrated herbal powders that are produced to exacting specifications using only natural extraction solutions, such as water and alcohol. Herb Pharm completely avoids the use of industrial solvents like hexane and methanol, which can leave synthetic residues in the finished product.

These encapsulated herbs are laboratory verified for purity and potency, are non-GMO and are suitable for vegans and vegetarians.

Topical Herbal Oils & Salve

Soothing and nourishing herbal preparations applied directly to the skin. Use them alone or in tandem with an ingestible extract to enhance your herbal experience.
