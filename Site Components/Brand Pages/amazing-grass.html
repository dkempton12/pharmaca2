About Amazing Grass

For more than 60 years, Amazing Grass has grown organic alfalfa and cereal grasses on a family farm in Kansas. Years of experience have led them to perfect the growing, harvesting, dehydration and storage of cereal grasses—ensuring optimal nutrition in some of the world’s most nutrient-dense Green SuperFoods.

In today’s world, many people do not receive their recommended servings of fruits and vegetables. Amazing Grass makes it easy and delicious with products that support a healthy energy level, immune functions, body alkalinity and a healthy digestive system.

Amazing Grass offers a variety of flavors and functions to meet your lifestyle and nutritional needs, including their classic Organic Wheat Grass powders, premium Raw Reserve, alkalizing Green Superfood and Amazing Meal with added protein and 5 billion probiotics.

Amazing Grass is dedicated to the finest and raw ingredients keeping the best of what Mother Nature has perfected.

Amazing Grass Organic Wheat Grass

Amazing Grass Organic Wheat Grass is one of the most potent leafy greens on Earth, not to mention a great source of vitamins and minerals. Farm fresh, USDA Certified, this 100% whole-leaf, organic wheat grass naturally detoxifies the body and boosts immune systems.

Amazing Grass grows its wheat grass at a family-run farm in Kansas, where it’s harvested at its nutritional peak just prior to the jointing stage, when they cut just the tip of the leaf–the most nutrient-rich part of the plant.
