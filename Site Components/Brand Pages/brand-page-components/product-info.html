Avéne Product Information

Avène Thermal Spring Water

Naturally soothing for sensitive skin since 1743. The key element in all Avène skin care products, its unique composition is clinically shown by more than 150 studies to calm inflammation, itching, redness and irritation while soothing and softening the skin.

Advanced Daily Sun Care

A complete range of high-protection sun care offering maximum sun protection that’s gentle enough for the most sensitive skin. Enhanced with a powerful antioxidant and soothing Avène Thermal Spring Water for long-lasting hydration. In two formulas: MINERAL, made with 100% minerals for hypersensitive and post-procedure skin, and CLASSIC, non-irritating chemical formulas for normal and sensitive skin.

Hypersensitive Skin

To give hypersensitive skin the safety it needs, Avène has created the concept of Sterile cosmetics—a unique process resulting in 100% sterile formulas and production. Products are made from minimal ingredients for maximum tolerance and enriched with patented Parcerine® to soothe irritation and redness and restore the skin’s barrier.

Cold Cream

Originally created in 150 AD by a Roman physician, Cold Cream has long been known for its moisturizing properties. Avène’s rich and moisturizing Cold Cream formula combines the purest ingredients, including Thermal Spring Water, pure white beeswax and pharmaceutical-grade paraffin oil to maintain the skin’s suppleness, heal abrasions and soothe the drying effects of environmental aggressors.

Hydrance Optimale

Dehydrated skin lacks water and appears dull, with uneven texture and fine, dry surface lines. Avène Hydrance Optimale is formulated to help prevent water evaporation from the skin, revealing a smoother and more luminous complexion.

Cleanance

Oily, blemish-prone skin is characterized by shine, excess sebum, enlarged pores and uneven skin texture. The Cleanance regimen gently purifies skin and removes excess sebum without drying or stripping the skin. Recommended by dermatologists to be used in conjunction with acne medications and treatments.

YsthéAL

YsthéAL is formulated with a patented combination of ingredients targeting the early signs of aging by smoothing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, restoring skin's radiance and protecting against free radicals.

TriXéra+ Selectiose

TriXéra+ Selectiose helps calm irritation and itch for very dry skin, including skin prone to atopic dermatitis and eczema. Its powerful combination of Selectiose, Lipidic-Trio, Glycine and Thermal Spring Water help soothe redness and dryness, prevent moisture loss and restore the skin's barrier.

Sérénage

Sérénage Nutri-Redensifying Care features a unique combination of ingredients that visibly plump and tone the skin while boosting vitality and comfort.

Cicalfate

A must-have for every family, Cicalfate® features a patented combination of ingredients—Sucralfate, a Copper-Zinc Complex and Avène Thermal Spring Water—clinically shown to help speed the skin recovery process by maintaining an optimal healing environment. Suitable for infants, children and adults.

Indications: burns, dry patches, stitches, non-oozing diaper rash, cuts, scrapes, cracked skin, perleche, peri-oral dermatitis, retroauricular fissure, post-procedure (peels/laser)
