About Oregon's Wild Harvest

Since 1994, Oregon’s Wild Harvest’s singular mission has been to produce the highest quality, whole herbal supplements available using practices that heal the earth while making products that nurture the body and soul.

As farmers and manufacturers, Oregon’s Wild Harvest delivers on the promise of environmental stewardship, purity and potency by doing what’s right every day. That means:

  • Farming their 387-acres following USDA Organic guidelines and practices
  • Procuring herbs that can’t be grown on their land from a community of trusted growers who can meet their stringent standards for quality.
  • Formulating whole herb products that retain the life, energy and vitality from nature. They believe the whole plant has everything you need.
  • Encouraging diversity—on their land, in the workplace and within their community.

When you choose Oregon’s Wild Harvest, you will feel good knowing…

  • That they’re an independent, family-owned and operated company that’s in it for the long haul!
  • They collect and replant our organic seeds. These seeds will become a source for quality medicinal plants for generations to come.
  • Every one of their products are scientifically tested to ensure quality and purity. In fact, their standards for quality control exceed those required by the FDA and their certifiers.
  • Their encapsulated herbal supplements are free of flowing agents like silicon dioxide and magnesium stearate.
  • Taking whole herb supplements connects you to life and nature. Your body will thank you.
