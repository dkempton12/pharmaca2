About Genexa

Genexa is the world’s first line of USDA Certified Organic and Non-GMO Project Verified homeopathic medicines for children and adults. Genexa was founded on the belief that medicine should not only be effective and unquestionably safe, but that it should be healthy, too.

Genexa is revolutionizing medicine by removing harsh, synthetic ingredients commonly found in remedies and replacing them with cleaner, healthier ingredients.

A medicine you can trust

Genexa is devoted to making medicine healthier, from sourcing the highest quality ingredients and using extensive research to formulate the most effective medicines to compliance with FDA and regulatory guidelines.

Committed to USDA Organic ingredients

Genexa is dedicated to being a certified USDA Organic company, which means all their medicines are made from certified organic ingredients and are rigorously tested through a highly audited process that examines the entire supply chain. This ensures Genexa products are free of toxins and unhealthy ingredients, including pesticides and artificial fillers, so they can commit to truly making medicine healthy.

A proud Non-GMO Verified company

Most medicines are full of potentially harmful genetically modified ingredients like lactose, corn syrup and flavorings derived from high-risk ingredients. Genexa prides themselves on sourcing only non-GMO ingredients. Each ingredient is transported separately and audited along the way to make sure integrity is maintained throughout the process.

Spread the Health

As a Certified B Corporation, Genexa we want to do our part to help make the world a healthier place. Their Spread the Health initiative was created to provide health services for kids in need. For each product purchased, Genexa donates to charitable organization focused on children’s health.
