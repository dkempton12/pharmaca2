About W3LL PEOPLE

Founded by an elite Beauty Dream Team comprised of a makeup guru, a cosmetic dermatologist and a treehugger, W3LL PEOPLE is taking green beauty to a prettier place.

Shirley Pinkson, renowned for her success as a corporate leader who shaped NARS Cosmetics into a global phenomenon, drives W3LL PEOPLE product development. Leveraging more than 20 years as a professional makeup artist, she designs pioneering products that deliver solutions the modern woman demands: simplicity, authenticity and performance.

This professional-grade natural and organic makeup works harder and faster so you don’t have to. It delivers extraordinary colors, coverage and healing protection in high-tech formulas—formulas so impossibly elegant, you’re barely aware of how gorgeously healthy you are!

  • Premium quality ingredients tested for superior performance
  • Commitment to sourcing from top-tier, local resources
  • Emphasis on organic botanicals rich in vitamins, antioxidants and moisturizing agents
  • No fillers, including propylene glycol and petrolatum
  • Ultra pigmented, high impact colors
  • Advanced delivery formulas move and breathe with skin
  • Innovative botanical complex prevents product from creasing or fading
  • All products tested in the W3LL PEOPLE Studio by their green beauty pros using exacting requirements
  • Awarded and endorsed the best in the business
  • All products are free from: cruelty, parabens, petrochemicals, glycols, silicones, sulphates, artificial dyes, artificial fragrance, GMOs and nanoparticles
