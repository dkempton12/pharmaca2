About Trilipiderm

Almost everyone experiences dry skin. The key to treating it starts with a basic understanding of skin and skin cell composition.

At Trilipid Research Institute, they understand the structure of skin. Their 40+ years of experience researching and testing ingredients has equipped them with valuable knowledge to create products for the treatment of skin.

Trilipiderm’s philosophy is simple: Create products focused on solving and preventing dry skin using only ingredients that mimic what’s found naturally in our skin.

Born in Jackson Hole, Wyo., where nature is at once its most amazing and most harsh, Trilipiderm’s line of dry skin moisturizers are tough enough to hold up in any condition and gentle enough to pamper skin.

Trilipididerm’s products:

  • Use active natural ingredients to moisturize, eliminate dryness and help prevent the return of dryness
  • Help form a natural boundary to seal in moisture and reduce Trans Epidermal Water Loss (TEWL)
  • Help replace essential lipids in the skin that have been lost due to use of strong soaps and detergents, or over-exposure to the sun
  • Improve the tone, texture, elasticity and appearance of the skin
  • Leave skin smooth and soft, with no rough, flaky surface
  • Relieve irritation
  • Help remove wrinkles
  • Are a source of omega-3 and omega-6 essential fatty acids
  • Are rich in vitamin E, an effective natural antioxidant and other vitamins
  • Are non-greasy and absorb quickly
  • Are non-comedogenic
  • Are free of parabens, petrolatum, mineral oil, silicone and phthalates
  • Are cruelty-free—not tested on animals
  • Are recommended by pharmacists
  • Can be used by men and women of all ages

About Frank Fanning, Trilipiderm Founder and CEO

As founder and CEO of a world-recognized company dedicated to the development of unique molecules for personal care use, Frank has spent more than 30 years in working with, and supplying, formula ingredients to the major cosmetic houses of the world. The insight and knowledge gained from his world-wide lectures to clients and trade groups, coupled with his personal formulating experiences, has afforded Frank immeasurable expertise in understanding the problems associated with skin care and aging, and the ability to strategically direct the development of combinations of ingredient technology to provide solutions to these problems. These solutions are the foundation of Trilipid Research Institute.
