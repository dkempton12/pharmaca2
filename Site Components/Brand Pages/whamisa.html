About Whamisa

This Korean-based brand offers a full line of skin care for both men and women and focuses on the benefits of natural botanic ingredients. They have dedicated more than 10 years to research and development, resulting in patented technology that sets their products apart from competitors.

Whamisa skin care products offer these key features

  • Patented skin care fermentation technology
  • Natural preservation, without synthetic preservatives
  • No purified water, but only botanical extracts from flowers, fruits, seeds, and root
  • Never tested on animals
  • Most products are vegan friendly
