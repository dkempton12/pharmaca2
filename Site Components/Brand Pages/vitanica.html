About Vitanica

Dr. Tori Hudson N.D. and her business partner Doug Stapf founded Vitanica in 1995 with the dream of providing women with effective and affordable natural health care options.

At the time the company was started, Dr. Hudson had 10 years of clinical experience to draw on, and realized there was a lack of readily available high quality nutritional/herbal supplements that specifically addressed women’s issues. She wanted to create products not only for her patients in her clinic, but also for health stores, to offer women affordable and reliable natural health care options available on the shelf.

Vitanica products serve women in three ways: by addressing topics that affect women directly, such as pregnancy, menstrual cycle care and menopause; addressing health concerns that tend to affect women more than men, such as sleep support, adrenal support, gastrointestinal support, urinary tract support, emotional and calming support; and addressing topics that affect women differently than men, like cardiovascular support. All Vitanica formulas are developed by Dr. Hudson and are based on expertise rooted in her clinical experience, her ongoing scientific research reviews and the time-honored tradition of natural medicine in women's health care.

Vitanica products are manufactured with the utmost care and are tested for accuracy, purity and potency. Only the highest quality ingredients go into the products, and loyal supporters, both professionals and consumers, have come to know and rely on the consistent quality Vitanica is known for. The products are made without binders, fillers, preservatives, artificial colors or flavorings. The entire line is 100% suitable for vegetarians, and the majority of the line is also suitable for vegans.

About Dr. Tori Hudson

Dr. Tori Hudson is a naturopathic physician who has specialized in women's health care for more than 31 years. She is a native Oregonian and an avid outdoors venturer, who—despite her busy schedule—always makes time for long walks in Forest Park or the city, for kayaking, camping and hiking. She is the Medical Director at her NW Portland clinic, A Woman's Time. She is also a clinical professor at the National College of Naturopathic Medicine in Portland, at SW College of Natural Medicine in Arizona and at Bastyr University in Washington. She has devoted her life to research, education and her clinical work in the interest of helping women reach better health. Dr. Hudson also does numerous lectures nationwide at professional conferences and public consumer lectures, as well as educational trainings for retailers. Dr. Hudson also serves on Pharmaca’s Integrative Health Advisory Board.

Her book, Women's Encyclopedia of Natural Medicine, now in its second edition, is regarded as a primary reference for women's health care and alternative medicine by both health care professionals and women seeking self-help and information. Dr. Hudson is also a regular contributing author to numerous articles, including the Townsend Letter and numerous natural health publications.

Vitanica is proud to partner with Pharmaca in an effort to achieve the original goal of helping more and more women learn about themselves and find effective and affordable natural health care options.
