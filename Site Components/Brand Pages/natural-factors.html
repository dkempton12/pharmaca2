ABOUT THE COMPANY

For more than 50 years, Natural Factors has supplied North American consumers with dietary supplements they trust to support their family's health. This family-owned Canadian company is headquartered in Coquitlam, British Columbia.

Natural Factors is one of the industry leaders in organic farming, sourcing and development of raw materials, research and development, quality control and quality assurance. With hundreds of acres of farmland in the fertile valleys of central British Columbia, Natural Factors cultivates a variety of medicinal crops for research as well as production. The company strives for the highest level of vertical integration, bringing as many processes in-house as possible, for maximum quality assurance and 100 percent control over the end product. Their manufacturing capabilities include tableting, soft gel and hard gel encapsulation and packaging, as well as organic farming of herbs.

The company's manufacturing facilities conform to all the requirements of Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) set out by the Canadian government's Natural Health Products Directorate (NHPD) as well as the FDA GMP for Dietary Supplements. In addition, the facility has been certified by the Therapeutic Goods Administration of Australia (TGA), considered one of the most stringent guidelines for manufacturing dietary supplements in the world.

Natural Factors has an extensive team of fully qualified scientists, including Dr. Michael Murray, who leads the R&D labs, Quality Control, Quality Assurance and Product Development groups. More than 50 scientists, many of whom are experts in their field, work in these and other departments to ensure that Natural Factors produces impeccable products guaranteed to be safe and efficacious.

Natural Factors is dedicated to establishing the highest standard of reliability and effectiveness for natural products, and total quality control.
