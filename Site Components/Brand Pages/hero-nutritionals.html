About Hero Nutritionals

Hero Nutritionals believes in embracing a healthy, happy life through positive nutrition. That’s why in 1997 they invented Yummi Bears® The Original® Gummy Vitamin, and in 2007 they created Slice of Life®, gummy vitamins specifically formulated for adults.

Hero Nutritionals is passionate about providing essential nutrients in delightfully yummy, fun-to-take gummies for the whole family that are made with natural ingredients and lots of love. For them, pure love deserves pure nutrition®.

Their Philosophy

Purity

To Hero Nutritionals, purity means creating vitamins that deliver the key nutrients bodies need to be healthy and happy, without any of the bad stuff like artificial colors, flavors, sweeteners or preservatives. That’s why their gummy vitamins are free of allergens, gluten and dairy, with nothing artificial added. Plus, they offer gummy vitamins for every dietary specialty, from organic and non-GMO to naturally sweetened sugar-free, and vegetarian options with a fruit pectin base!

Positivity

At Hero, positivity is a way of life. Hero consists of a group of people who work hard, always look on the bright side, remember to stop and enjoy the moment, of course, and ensure their gummy vitamins spread only good vibes! That’s why they say that love is their key ingredient—it’s at the core of their complete line of gummy vitamins for the whole family!

Potency

In 1997, Hero cracked the code on how to get the wholesome nutrition kids need in a yummy gummy by creating the first ever line of gummy vitamins. To this day Hero ensures their gummy vitamins are both nutritionally potent and delightfully yummy. Hero’ gummy vitamins are lab tested to ensure purity and potency.
