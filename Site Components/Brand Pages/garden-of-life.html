About Garden of Life

Garden of Life: A Culture of Empowerment

For more than 14 years, Garden of Life’s mission has been Empowering Extraordinary Health®. The human body craves the nutrition found in food, and Garden of Life's goal is to feed that need with cleanest, highest quality whole-food ingredients possible. No synthetic nutrients, artificial flavors, sweeteners or chemical preservatives are ever added to their products.

Key Benefits

  • Clean whole food ingredients
  • Certified USDA Organic (no toxic pesticides or residues)
  • Non-GMO Project Verified products (Independent 3rd Party Certification)
  • Innovative, science-driven formulas

Garden of Life’s commitment to health goes beyond offering some of the most effective nutritional products in the world. Of equal importance is their quest to preserve the integrity of the planet through eco-friendly initiatives and by supporting relevant philanthropic organizations throughout the world in desperate need of help.

A Culture of Sustainability

Since their inception, Garden of Life has been committed to protecting the environment and seeking out best practices to achieve that goal. From office operations to manufacturing and distribution processes, Garden of Life takes pride in protecting the planet by participating and adhering—without compromise—to the standards of the following organizations:

  • The Forest Stewardship Council
  • Green-e

A Culture of Giving

An estimated 190 million children under age 5 suffer from vitamin A deficiency, a major underlying cause of child mortality. Vitamin Angels is dedicated to eliminating under-five child mortality in at-risk populations by providing children with the necessary vitamin A. Garden of Life has been a major force behind Vitamin Angels’ quest since 2009.

Garden of Life also partners with SOS Children’s Villages International, raising funds to build housing in Peru for at-risk children who can no longer be raised with their biological families. Additional well-deserving organizations Garden of Life supports include: The Organic Center, WomenHeart and the National Parkinson Foundation.

Garden of Life’s values are simple, and they are thankful for their family’s genuine and caring commitment to humanity, dignity and to the endless pursuit of a cleaner, healthier planet.
