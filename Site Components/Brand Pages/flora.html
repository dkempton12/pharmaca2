About Flora

Family Owned & Operated

Flora is owned and operated by Thomas Greither, a third-generation health entrepreneur who follows in the footsteps of his father and grandfather. His grandfather, Mr. Otto Greither, was founder of the world-famous Salus-Haus in Germany in 1916; his father, Mr. Otto Greither, Jr., dedicated his life to the discovery, production and distribution of natural health care products. Flora has preserved this dedication by perfecting herbal remedies that deliver maximum purity, quality and potency.

Standards & Certifications

All of Flora's products are manufactured in accordance with GMP standards; Flora’s facilities and manufacturing procedures are in compliance with all regulatory agencies in Canada and the United States, including Health Canada and the US Food and Drug Administration (FDA). In addition, Flora is a certified organic producer and processor overseen by the third-party certifying agency Quality Assurance International, (QAI). This means that Flora’s organic herbal remedies are guaranteed top quality and chemical free, from the seed that is planted to the harvest, and from the post-harvest facilities to final production and packing. Flora also partnered with the Non-GMO Project in 2012 as part of its ongoing commitment to providing the highest quality supplements available.

Environmental Responsibility

Flora’s dedication to preserving the environment consists of three key initiatives:

  • The use of the most ecologically friendly packaging for products
  • Working with local farmers to obtain ecologically sustainable supplies of high quality, botanical raw materials
  • Eco-friendly production policies and procedures

About Flora Products

Salus Haus…Celebrating 100 Years of Excellence & Environmental Consciousness

Salus-Haus manufactures some of the most well-known and trusted herbal products on Earth, one of which is the award-winning Floradix® liquid iron formula. Striving for the highest standards of product quality and environmental stewardship, Salus-Haus has committed to several strict guidelines, which meet and exceed all European pharmaceutical production standards. These include using only organic farming practices and excluding genetically modified ingredients, artificial colors and preservatives. Salus-Haus also tests all raw herbs for pesticides, heavy metals and other residues, and ensures all facilities are Kosher.

Dr. Dunner Swiss Formulas

Flora and Dr. Dünner are more than just partners in health; they have a bond that is stronger than business. Peter Greither, owner of Dr. Dünner, is also the brother of Thomas Greither. Dr. Dünner, based in Switzerland, manufactures 100s of products that are sold throughout the world. The long-standing business relationship between Flora and Dr. Dünner enables consumers in North America to purchase Swiss-quality products such as Sambu®Guard & Sambu Elderberry Concentrate.

Udo’s Choice

Founded in 1994, the Udo's Choice line of nutrition products created an entirely new standard for the food and supplement industries. The Udo’s Choice line is fully committed to delivering only the very highest quality blends of oils, probiotics, digestive enzymes, greens and fiber. All plant-based, organic and Non-GMO.
