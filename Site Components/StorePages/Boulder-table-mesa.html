South Boulder Staff

Julie M., MS, RD, Assistant Manager

Julie is a certified herbalist and holds a Master's degree in nutrition from the University of Bridgeport in Connecticut. She has been involved in the field of nutrition for 20 years, having started work in a conservative hospital setting as a registered dietitian. Upon completion of her herbal certification she began her career in complementary health and worked for years in research and development for a professional line of nutrition supplements. She has instructed many online nutrition classes and has continued a private practice throughout her career. Her business, The Gluten Free Bistro, is the culmination of years of nutrition experience and living gluten-free combined with a genuine desire to provide a nutritious product for the celiac and gluten-intolerant communities.

Tami O., Nutritionist

Tami began working with natural and alternative medicine 20 years ago at a natural food store in New Mexico, where she was the buyer and manager of the natural medicine department. Her interest in health and nutrition continued after a move to England, where she earned her Nutritional Therapy Certification from The College of Naturopathic Medicine in London. She has done talks and presentations on the importance of digestive health and detoxification both in England and America.

Tania S., Nutritionist

Tania earned a Master Nutrition Therapist (MNT) degree from the Nutrition Therapy Institute in Denver. She has a background in the social services, youth work and in counseling families. When changing careers, she chose nutrition therapy as a means of bringing her counseling experience together with health and wellness. Through her personal health journey, she resonated most closely with the holistic approach. Tania has learned the value of a healthy diet and lifestyle as well as the value of challenging long-time habits and beliefs about food and its connection to wellness. She also has a private practice in Longmont, Holistic Health and Healing.
